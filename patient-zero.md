Setup a team of agents to get this to production.
A Product Owner, Architect, Lead software developer,  UX designer and QA Tester

The architect and product owner use the Opus model
The UX designer, Lead developer, and QA Tester use the Sonnet model.

The product brief is in product-brief.md
Lets define how the team works together.

The product owner and ux-designer get together to discuss high level product interactions, look and feel and market fit.

Then the Architect interprets their output to come up with a technical solution.
If the architect has any question he should ask the UX designer or the Product owner.

The execution team use efficient kanban method.
After the initial product brief is completed the loop can be repeated to add new features.

The architect delegates implementation to the lead developer who implements according to 
the latest and greatest best practices for the architecture.

After the lead dev is done with implementation the qa tester validates everything from a devils advocate point of view.
QA develops IDEMPOTENT reusable scripts to deploy the product to a stable environment.
perform local mocked testing using the latest and best fitting tools.
Pre prodcution backend API integration testing AND Front end UI testing in Chrome in a live staging environment is performed before final acceptance to promote to production.

Production and staging environments, deployment and testing methods will be defined by the architect during initial kickoff with the product owner and ux designer.
